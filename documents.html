Standard Engagement Documents

All JUA Solution engagements are governed by California law with exclusive jurisdiction in Orange County, CA. Documents are reviewed annually and updated as needed.

MSA · 2026 Legal Edition
Master Services Agreement

The foundational contract governing all professional consulting engagements with JUA Solution LLC. Covers service scope, independent contractor status, IP ownership, confidentiality obligations, payment terms (Net 15), late payment interest, limitation of liability, non-solicitation, and termination procedures. Required for all engagements.

📄 9 pages 📍 California law · Orange County jurisdiction ⚖️ Required before work begins

SOW · 2026 Legal Edition
Statement of Work

Defines the specific scope, deliverables, timeline, billing model, project assumptions, and client responsibilities for each individual engagement. Executed alongside the MSA. Covers D365 F&O, EDI, managed services, and all related consulting work. Change requests, acceptance procedures, and suspension rights are also defined here.

📄 6 pages 📋 Covers D365 F&O, EDI, Managed Services ⚖️ Required per engagement

MNDA · 2026 Legal Edition
Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement

A mutual confidentiality agreement that protects both parties during early-stage discussions, scoping conversations, and any exchange of sensitive business information — before a formal engagement begins. Recommended for all new client relationships prior to sharing proprietary data, system access, or business process details.
