Who We Are

About JUA Solution

A specialized Microsoft and EDI consulting firm headquartered in Irvine, California — built by practitioners, for enterprise clients who demand depth over breadth.

Our Story

Built on Deep Expertise, Focused on Results

JUA Solution LLC was founded with a single conviction: enterprise technology consulting should be led by people who have actually implemented the systems they advise on — not generalists reading documentation for the first time alongside a client.

Our practice is deliberately focused. We work exclusively within the Microsoft ecosystem and EDI space because that focus is what allows us to go deeper, move faster, and avoid the costly mistakes that come from surface-level knowledge.

Every engagement is staffed with certified consultants who have delivered real projects — in real enterprise environments — under the same pressures, deadlines, and constraints our clients face every day.
